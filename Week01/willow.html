Willow branches have been used for a long period of time by the Native Americans and many such other people as a natural remedy for pain. Simply chew on the branch or its leaves and the pain will begin to melt away. This is a great alternative for those who are allergic or overly-sensitive to most modern pain killers. Although, willow trees can be hard to find there fore the price is a little higher.
